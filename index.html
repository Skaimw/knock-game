How To Play
Knock — The Malawian Card Game
🎯
The Objective
Be the first player to empty your hand.

When you're down to 1 card, you must announce ✋ KNOCK to warn the other players.
🃏
Playing a Card
On your turn, play a card that matches the suit or rank of the top discard card.

If you can't play, tap the Draw Pile to draw one card and end your turn.
✨
Special Cards
A Ace — Change the active suit to anything you choose.

J Jack — Skip the next player's turn.

2 Two — Next player must draw 2 cards.

8 Eight — Play on any suit. See next slide →
8️⃣
The 8 Card
An 8 can be played on any suit. But if you play it alone with no support, you draw 1 card as a penalty.

Play an 8 + a same-suit card as a combo to escape the penalty and trigger the 8's effect.
🔄
Counter the 2
If someone plays a 2 on you, you can play your own 2 to cancel it — the penalty disappears and the next player plays freely.

If you have no 2, tap the Draw Pile to take your 2 cards.
🔃
Reversals
Playing an odd number of 8s in a combo reverses the direction of play.

Watch the spinning arrow in the center of the table — it shows which way the turn is moving.
🏆
The Tournament
Every round costs K500 ante. The round winner takes the whole pot.

Players with K0 are eliminated. Last player standing wins the tournament.
🎲
You're Ready!
Pick a card to decide who goes first — lowest value wins.
